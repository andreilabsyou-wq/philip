Why Choose RHMC

Anchored in Excellence

At RHMC, we go beyond academics — we shape future maritime professionals with discipline, skill, and purpose.

01
MARINA-Aligned Training

Programs designed to meet Maritime Industry Authority of the Philippines standards — certified and career-ready upon graduation.

02
Career Exit Pathway

Graduates receive both an academic diploma and a defined career pathway — a direct route into the maritime industry right after SHS.

03
Experienced Faculty

Learn from educators and maritime practitioners who bring real-world experience into the classroom, bridging theory and practice.

04
State-of-the-Art Facilities

RHMC invests in maritime training equipment and simulation labs for hands-on practice before stepping onto an actual vessel.

05
Montessori Values

Our Montessori foundation fosters independence, critical thinking, and a love of learning — qualities that set our graduates apart.

06
Global Demand for Seafarers

The Philippines is the world's top supplier of maritime personnel. Starting in SHS puts you ahead in a highly rewarding global career.
